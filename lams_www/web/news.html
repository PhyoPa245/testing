Welcome to LAMS 4.0 Demo!

New features:

Integrated Question Bank!

All your assessment questions in a single place! Reuse your questions and perform analytics on your assessments across lesson. Questions now have versions so you can track their performance with powerful analytics thru out the question lifespan!

Learning Outcomes

You can now map your curriculum and learning outcomes to all activities in your  learning design. Also assess students performance directly to the learning outcomes. 

New doKu tool

The new doKu tool is an online document editor providing collaborative editing in real-time. Students can collaboratively share a working document to create ideas while teachers can observe their contributions as they happen. 

New Peer Review tool

The most versatile Peer and Self Review tool you will find. Allow students to rate, rank and/or comments on their own and others work/performance.

New password gate

Now you can control your lesson gates using passwords. Only when you provide the gate's password the students can proceed to the rest of the lesson. 

Embed rich media, images, videos directly into any HTML editor

If you use external content in your teaching, now LAMS makes it easier to embed content from websites like Youtube, TED, Wikipedia, Flickr, Vimeo, Twitter, Instagram, Pintrest, Facebook, Prezi, GitHub, and many more!

IMS LTI 1.3 integrations

LAMS is now an IMS LTI 1.3 tool provider and consumer, allowing LAMS to be integrated with any Learning Management System.
